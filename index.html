APOGEE CALCULATOR

Introducing the Apogee Calculator: the brainchild of our grand ambitions, but ultimately humbled by the complexities of rocket science. Our team spent most of the time designing the UI and attempting to craft a witty preamble, but don't worry, we made sure to leave enough time for the actual calculator (sort of). We simplified the rocket to a particle moving in a vacuum to calculate the optimal engine type based only on the mass and total impulse class, but please note that it's only applicable to Estes Model Rockets. Although this design doesn't quite meet our objectives for accuracy, universal applicability, and extensiveness, it's still super easy to use. Just click the card to flip it, and let the fun (and liability) begin! Disclaimer: The creators of this system are not responsible for any potential complications that may arise as a result of using this calculator. But hey, nothing ventured, nothing blasted off, right?
